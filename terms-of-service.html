📋 2. Description of Service

Flock Manager is a browser-based church administration application that enables authorised church administrators to:

👤 Member Management

Create and maintain member profiles, contacts, ministry roles, and leadership records.

💰 Giving Records

Record tithes, offerings, welfare contributions, and other financial giving by type.

📅 Attendance Tracking

Log service attendance by gender and age group; view trends and summaries.

☁ Backup & Sync

Export JSON backups to your device or sync encrypted data to your own Google Drive.

The App runs client-side in your browser. All church records are stored in your device's IndexedDB and/or your own Google Drive account. KISSWARD MULTIMEIA does not operate a central server that stores congregation data.
