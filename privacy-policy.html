👁 1. Overview

Plain-language summary: Flock Manager is a browser-based church administration tool. Your church's member records stay in your own browser (IndexedDB) and optionally in your own Google Drive. We do not operate a central server that stores your congregation's personal data.

This Privacy Policy explains how KISSWARD MULTIMEIA ("we", "our", or "us") handles information when you use Flock Manager ("the App"), accessible at https://flockmanager.github.io/flockmanager/. By using the App, you agree to the practices described here.

📦 2. Data We Collect

Flock Manager operates primarily offline. Data falls into two categories:

CategoryExamplesWhere Stored
Member Records Names, contact details, photos, ministry roles, next-of-kin Your device (IndexedDB) / your Google Drive
Payment Records Tithes, offerings, welfare contributions, dates Your device (IndexedDB) / your Google Drive
Attendance Records Service dates, headcounts (males/females/children) Your device (IndexedDB) / your Google Drive
Authentication Data Google account name, email address, profile picture URL Your browser's localStorage (session only)
Preferences Theme (light/dark), selected currency Your browser's localStorage
Analytics Aggregate usage events (via Firebase Analytics) Google's Firebase servers — anonymised

We do not collect payment card information, passwords, government ID numbers, or any sensitive financial credentials.

⚙️ 3. How We Use Data

Information collected is used solely to:

  • Operate and display church member, payment, and attendance records within the App.
  • Authenticate your session via Google Sign-In so only authorised administrators access the workspace.
  • Sync your data to your own Google Drive AppData folder (optional, at your request).
  • Understand aggregate usage patterns to improve the App's reliability and features (Firebase Analytics — no personally identifiable information is shared).

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any commercial purpose beyond operating the App.

🔒 4. Storage & Security

All church records are stored exclusively in:

  • IndexedDB — a browser-native database on your own device. Data never leaves your browser unless you explicitly export a backup or enable Google Drive sync.
  • Your Google Drive AppData folder — if you grant Drive permission. Only you (and authorised users of the same Google account) can access this folder. KISSWARD MULTIMEIA cannot read or write to it.

We recommend restricting App access to trusted administrators and signing out after each session. Because data lives on your device, you are responsible for maintaining physical and account security of the devices on which the App is used.

🔑 5. Google Services

Flock Manager integrates the following Google services:

  • Google Sign-In (OAuth 2.0) — Used to authenticate you. We receive your name, email, and profile photo from Google upon sign-in. This data is stored only in your browser's localStorage for the duration of your session.
  • Google Drive AppData API — Used to save and restore backup files in a private, App-specific folder in your Google account. Your Drive files are not accessible to us.
  • Firebase Authentication & Analytics — Used for sign-in management and anonymous usage analytics. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

Use of these services is subject to Google's Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

🤝 6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data or your church's member records with any third party, except:

  • Google — as described in Section 5 above, solely to provide authentication and optional Drive sync features.
  • Legal obligations — if required to do so by law or a valid legal process.

🗑 7. Data Retention

Because records are stored on your device and in your own Google Drive, you control retention entirely. You may:

  • Delete individual member, payment, or attendance records from within the App.
  • Clear your browser's site data to remove all App data from your device.
  • Delete the Flock Manager backup file from your Google Drive AppData folder via Google Account Permissions.

Firebase Analytics data is retained in accordance with Google's data retention policies and is not linked to individual users.

✅ 8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data we hold. Because we hold no server-side copy of your church records, most rights can be exercised directly within the App. For any residual data (e.g. analytics), contact us at the address below.

🧒 9. Minors

Flock Manager is an administrative tool intended for use by adult church administrators. Minors should not create accounts or use the App independently. Records of minor congregation members may be entered by authorised adult administrators in the course of legitimate church administration, subject to applicable laws and the church's own data protection policies.
