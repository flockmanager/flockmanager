Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 25, 2026

Flock Manager is provided by KISSWARD MULTIMEIA to help churches manage member records, giving records, attendance, reports, and backups.

Information We Collect

The app may store information entered by authorized users, including names, contact details, membership details, ministry information, attendance records, payment records, notes, and uploaded member photos. When you sign in with Google, the app receives basic Google profile information such as your name and email address.

How Data Is Stored

Flock Manager saves data locally in the user's browser first. If the user grants Google Drive permission, the app also saves and retrieves a backup file named Flock Manager Data.json in the signed-in Google account's app data storage.

Google Drive Access

The app requests the Google Drive app data permission only to create, update, and read its own backup file. The app does not request access to browse all files in the user's Google Drive.

How We Use Information

  • To display and manage church administration records in the app.
  • To save backups and restore data for the signed-in user.
  • To support app operation, troubleshooting, and security.

Analytics

The app may use Microsoft Clarity to understand general usage and improve the product. Analytics tools may collect technical information such as browser type, page interactions, and device information.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Data entered into the app is intended for use by the church or organization operating the app. Google Drive data remains connected to the Google account used for sign-in.

Data Control

Users can export backups, save data to their PC, and manage access through their Google account permissions. Removing app access from a Google account may stop future Drive backups.
